Synopsis by Maggie Sadler

A young teen named June (Storm Reid) finds her nightmare confirmed when her mother (Nia Long) inexplicably vanishes while on a Colombian vacation with her boyfriend. When the authorities thwart her quest for answers, June then relies on the contemporary technology at her disposal to know the truth, encountering a web of secrets about her mother that she struggles to fathom. Nicholas D. Johnson and Will Merrick co-direct the standalone sequel to the 2018 film. Co-starring Daniel Henney and Ken Leung.
