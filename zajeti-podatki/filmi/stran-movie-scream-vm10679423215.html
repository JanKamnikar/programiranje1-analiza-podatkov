Synopsis by Jinko Labitag

After twenty-five peaceful years, Woodsboro once again faces Ghostface, the entity that previously shook the small and quiet town with its brutal crimes. As the new perpetrator behind the mask goes after everyone related to the original murderers, they begin to open old wounds that have been forcefully tucked away in the past. Sam Carpenter (Melissa Barrera) then enlists the help of the town’s former Sheriff, Dewey Riley (David Arquette), and it doesn’t take long until Sidney Prescott (Neve Campbell) returns to face her old enemy. Matt Bettinelli-Olpin and Tyler Gillett co-direct this horror.
