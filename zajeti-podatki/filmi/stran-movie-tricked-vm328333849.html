Synopsis by Violet LeVoit

Paul Verhoeven directed this experimental film based on a crowd-sourced screenplay, interweaving a thriller about a businessman (Peter Blok) cornered by blackmailers and a pregnant former mistress (Sallie Harmsen) with behind-the-scenes footage of how he and hundreds of collaborators made the movie happen.
