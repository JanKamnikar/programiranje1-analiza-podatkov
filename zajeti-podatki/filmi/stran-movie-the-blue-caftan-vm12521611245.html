Synopsis by Farida Ezzat

Written and directed by Maryam Touzani, the drama film centers on a married couple running a caftan store in Morocco. Halim (Saleh Bakri) and Mina (Lubna Azabal) lead a quiet domestic life. They run their caftan store together but require more help with customers. They hire a young man named Youssef (Ayoub Missioui) as a store clerk. Soon, Youssef begins to shift the couple’s relationship dynamics. Co-starring Fouzia Ejjawi.
